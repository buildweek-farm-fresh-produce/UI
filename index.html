Farm Fresh Produce

Our Mission Statement:

Fresher food for a healthier future.

About Farm Fresh Produce:

We aim to bring produce to the general public straight from the farm!

Farmers post their inventory and consumers can access their local farmers inventory and purchase straight from them.

How To Get Started:

Click on the Farmer button if you are a Farmer or click the Consumer button if you are a Consumer. Then you will be able to either view requests for your produce as a farmer and sell your inventory or you will be able to view past orders as a consumer and make new orders.
